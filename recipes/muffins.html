Muffins

A yummy muffin recipe!

It's an easy to make recipe that can be topped with a range of toppings!

Ingredients

  • 220g plain flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 115g unsalted butter
  • 150g light brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 120g sour cream or yoghurt
  • 1 1/2 tsp pure vanilla extract
  • 60ml (1/4 cup) milk
  • Chosen toppings

Recipe

  1. Preheat oven to 218 degrees.
  2. Prepare muffin tray and set aside.
  3. In a large bowl, whisk the flour, baking powder, baking soda, cinnamon, and split together and set aside.
  4. In a large bowl beat the butter until smooth and creamy.
  5. Add the brown sugar and beat on high until creamed then scrape down the sides and bottom of the bowl.
  6. Add the eggs, sour cream, and vanilla extract.
  7. Mix until the mixture is combined and creamy. Scrape down the sides and bottom of the bowl as needed.
  8. Pour the dry ingredients into the wet ingredients and mix until just combined. Add in chosen toppings. Add the milk and continue to beat on low until combined.
  9. Spoon the batter evenly into each cup, filling to just over halfway. Sprinkle granulated sugar on top before baking.
  10. Bake the muffins at 425 degrees for 5 minutes then turn the temperature down to 177 degrees. Bake for an additional 10 - 15 minutes or until a toothpick inserted comes out clean. Rotate the tray every so often.
  11. Allow the muffins to cool for 5 minutes in the muffin tray then transfer to a wire rack to continue cooling.
