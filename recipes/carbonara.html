Carbonara

A traditional carbonara dish

This is a traditional recipe that only requires 5 ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Parmesan
  • Black pepper
  • Egg
  • Guanciale

Recipe

  1. Mix the egg yolk, parmesan, and black pepper together until you get a thick consistency. Set aside.
  2. Get your pasta cooking. Cook till al-dente.
  3. Fry guanciale for a few minutes then remove from heat.
  4. Once the pasta is cooked, add some pasta water to your egg and cheese mix, and add the pasta to the pan with the guanciale.
  5. On a high heat, cook the pasta and guanciale for a few minutes.
  6. Remove from heat, add sauce to pan and mix until pasta is coated.
  7. Serve immediately. Season with black pepper if necessary.
